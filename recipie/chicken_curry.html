chicken curry

ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 200 ml coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for another 2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add curry powder, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Stir well to combine with the onion mixture.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the pan. Cook until they are browned on all sides.
  5. Pour in the pureed tomatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  6. Add coconut milk and salt to taste. Stir well and bring to a simmer.
  7. Cover the pan and let it cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
